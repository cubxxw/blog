# Crawl budget. Paginated list pages carry no unique content and accounted for
# 543 of the site's 1,258 built URLs — GSC showed 445 of them sitting in
# "Discovered – currently not indexed", i.e. Google spending its budget queueing
# list pages while real articles waited. Blocking them costs nothing in
# discovery: /archives/ is a complete, unpaginated index of every post, and
# sitemap.xml carries all of them.
